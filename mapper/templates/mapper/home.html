Welcome to Dropped-Kerb Mapper

The Dropped-Kerb Mapper helps users record the location and condition of dropped kerbs in the UK. This data contributes to creating accessible routes for wheeled mobility device users.

How to Contribute

When you have created an account and signed in, you can

  • Create a new dropped kerb report
  • View your existing dropped kerb report
  • View all dropped kerb reports on a map
