Welcome to Dropped-Kerb Mapper

The purpose of Dropped-Kerb Mapper is to record the location and condition of dropped kerbs in the UK. This data contributes to creating accessible routes for wheeled mobility device users in the web application Mobility Mapper. Please note that Mobility Mapper is a work in progress, and we will keep all contributers up-to-date on its progress.

The data collected will also be used to raise the awareness of the need for well designed and maintained dropped kerbs in the UK, and to help local councils and authorities to understand where they are needed most.

How to Contribute

When you have created an account and signed in, you can

  • Create new dropped kerb reports by clicking on our map.
  • View your existing dropped kerb reports.

When you create an account, we ask if you use a wheeled mobility device. This is to help us better understand the data we collect, and how it can be used to improve the accessibility of the UK. We will not share your personal information with anyone else.
